Welcome to Kelanese! Kelahahuei ot kiekae!

A preliminary reference grammar based on fieldwork across Kelan.
Draft • Field Notes • Version 0.1

Kelanese functions as the primary lingua franca of the island nation of Kelan. While numerous regional and community-based varieties exist, Kelanese serves as the principal medium of interregional communication.

This reference grammar presents a descriptive account of Kelanese as attested through sustained observational fieldwork. Analyses herein reflect consistently observed patterns but remain provisional, pending further data and comparative investigation.

Scope of the Grammar

This work is intended as a descriptive reference grammar. It is not a pedagogical textbook, nor does it attempt to prescribe a single “correct” form of the language. Variation is treated as an inherent and structurally meaningful component of Kelanese.
